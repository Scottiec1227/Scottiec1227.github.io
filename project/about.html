Get to know me

Modern. Natural. Beautiful.

Hi, I’m Elizabeth! Originally from Washington, I now call beautiful Utah home. I’ve been working with flowers since 2018 and turned my passion into my own business in 2023. I’m inspired by nature and committed to creating arrangements that are more sustainable! When I’m not designing flowers, I’m spending time with my husband and our sweet dog, exploring the great outdoors, or binging a ghost hunting tv show. I’m so excited to work with you!
